Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our treatments, procedures, and services to help you make informed health decisions.

What conditions can acupuncture treat?

Acupuncture is effective in treating various conditions such as chronic pain, migraines, stress, digestive issues, insomnia, and muscle tension. It stimulates the body's healing process and improves overall well-being.

What is the difference between hijama (wet cupping) and dry cupping?

Hijama, or wet cupping, involves making small incisions on the skin to remove toxins, while dry cupping uses suction only to stimulate blood flow and relieve tension without breaking the skin.

Are laser treatments safe for all skin types?

Yes, our advanced laser technology is suitable for all skin types. Our professionals will assess your skin and hair conditions to ensure the treatment is safe and effective for your unique needs.

How many hair treatment sessions are needed for results?

Results vary depending on individual conditions. Typically, 4-6 sessions of micro-needling or derma roller treatments are recommended to see noticeable improvements in hair growth and thickness.

What physiotherapy treatments do you offer?

We offer a variety of physiotherapy treatments, including manual therapy, exercise therapy, and rehabilitation programs designed to improve mobility, strength, and overall function. These programs are personalized based on individual needs.
